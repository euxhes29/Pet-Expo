Who we are

Welcome to Pet Expo, your number one source for all things pets. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of pet information, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2021, Pet Expo has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for helping pet owners drove us to do tons of research, so that Pet Expo can offer you the world's most advanced pet platform. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We offer a wide range of information on different types of pets including cats, dogs, birds, and more. Our mission is to provide comprehensive guides, tips, and advice to help pet owners take the best care of their furry friends.

At Pet Expo, we believe that pets are part of the family. That's why we work hard to create content that is not only informative but also engaging and fun. From the latest pet news to health and wellness tips, we have everything you need to keep your pets happy and healthy.

We hope you enjoy our platform as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Pet Expo is one of the biggest events for animal lovers in Albania. Organized by animal enthusiasts, we aim to provide a wonderful experience for animal lovers and professionals.
